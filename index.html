Meet Miriam

From Zurich. To your dog's hair.

Miriam Lenz arrived in the desert from Zurich, Switzerland — formally trained in fashion and interior design before she traded couture clients for the four-legged kind.

Fourteen years grooming. Six-time titled Best Groomer in the Coachella Valley. Three professional grooming awards. A by-appointment-only salon she opened on South Palm Canyon Drive in 2016 — bright, calm, no kennel waits, every pup treated like the VIP they are.

She's not just trimming. She's reading the dog. Watching for moles. Choosing the soap the face can take. Talking with you about the cut before she touches a single hair.

"I make sure every pet leaves happier than they walked in — and most of them already came in pretty happy."
The Treatments

Spa-day energy. Show-ring results.

All-natural shampoos and conditioners. State-of-the-art equipment. Pre-groom consult, post-service follow-up. Call to book any combination.

Hydro Bath

Our Hydro Surge system uses InjectAir technology to combine fresh water, shampoo, and oxygen — penetrating the coat down to the skin. Up to 70% less bathing time. Zero hand scrubbing.

A tear-less, lick-safe face wash + color brightener — fresh blueberries, avocado, coconut oil, and aloe. Particularly nice for facial folds, tear-staining, and sensitive eyes.
